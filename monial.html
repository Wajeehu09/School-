About Us

Monial Iqra Al Arobiy Wal Islamic is dedicated to nurturing students in a holistic Islamic environment. Our mission is to provide quality education while instilling strong moral values and Islamic teachings.

Admissions

Admissions are open for the academic year. Parents are encouraged to visit the school and meet our staff. Enrollment forms can be collected from the school office or downloaded online.

Academics

Our curriculum integrates Islamic studies with modern education. We offer subjects ranging from Quran and Hadith to Science, Math, and English, promoting both religious and academic excellence.
